## Standard 5.  Artificial Intelligence (AI)

### Grade Level Expectation CS.3.5.1:  AI tools have the potential to affect society in a number of ways, and their design should take into account the varying needs of users to ensure usability across populations.

#### Prepared Graduates

11.  Evaluate the uses of AI.

#### Evidence Outcomes

##### Students Can:

1. Describe how AI-powered services are used in daily life.
2. Identify changes in how sectors of society operate due to the introduction of AI.
3. Describe how a job will change due to the introduction of AI or robotic technologies.

#### Academic Contexts and Connections

##### Colorado Essential Skills:

1. Identify and explain multiple perspectives when evaluating how AI can impact people differently. (Civic/Interpersonal Skills, Global / Cultural Awareness)
2. Analyze how deep fake media messages are constructed. (Professional Skills (Information Literacy)

##### Computer Science Practices:

1. Develop and Using Abstractions
2. Communicating about Computing
3. Fostering an Inclusive Computing Culture
